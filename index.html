About

3rd Annual PDMT

The AMSA-MAMS Pi Day Mathematics Tournament is a math competition run by high school students for middle school students. Teams may consist of up to five middle school students from the same school. The cost of participation is $10 per student. Lunch will be provided free of charge, and there will be prizes! Come celebrate Pi Day with us!

Partner

Massachusetts Academy of Math and Science


"Mass Academy is a public school in Massachusetts for advanced 11th and 12th grade students. Students take rigorous junior year classes and are enrolled as full time students at WPI as seniors."

Partner

Advanced Math and Science Academy


"AMSA is a public charter school in Massachusetts for middle and high school students. AMSA strives to create an atmosphere that celebrates knowledge, where children of all backgrounds and abilities excel."

Silver Sponsor

RSM-MetroWest (Framingham)


"RSM-MetroWest (Framingham) is an affiliate of Russian School of Mathematics, an after-school K–12 math enrichment program recently featured in NPR and the Atlantic magazine as one of the key players in the “Math Revolution”, and ranked one of the best schools in the world by the Johns Hopkins Center for Talented Youth. RSM helps children of all levels build a solid math foundation and develop their critical-thinking and problem-solving skills."

Bronze Sponsor

Math Altitude


"MathAltitude School of Mathematics is an after-school and weekend math enrichment school offering comprehensive mathematical education for students from preschool to high school. Our program motivates students to excel in their regular math classes by building their confidence and understanding of math concepts. From helping students to understand the basic mathematical concepts to developing creativity in solving complex real-life problems, our goal is to encourage logical reasoning, analytical thinking and independent learning."
